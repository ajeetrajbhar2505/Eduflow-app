Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 24, 2023

Welcome to ExamPro. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This policy outlines how we handle your data during online examinations to ensure academic integrity and a secure testing environment.

1. Information Collection

We collect information that you provide directly to us when you create an account, including your name, email address, and student ID. During examinations, we may collect:

  • check_circle Biometric verification data for identity confirmation.
  • check_circle Real-time video and audio feeds for proctoring purposes.
  • check_circle Device information including IP address and OS version.

2. Data Usage

The data collected is used strictly for the following purposes:

"We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Data is used exclusively for academic validation and reporting to your educational institution."

Proctoring data is automatically deleted after 60 days unless your institution requests a review for suspected academic misconduct.

3. Your Rights

As a student, you have the following rights regarding your data:

visibility

Access & Portability

Request a copy of your stored data.

delete

Data Deletion

Request removal of non-academic records.

edit

Rectification

Correct inaccurate personal information.

4. Security Measures

We implement industry-standard encryption (AES-256) for all data in transit and at rest. Access to exam recordings is restricted to authorized proctors and your institution's administrators.

Questions?

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out to our legal team.
